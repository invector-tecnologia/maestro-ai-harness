# Quality Assurance

## Purpose
Protect delivery quality through verification strategy

## Responsibilities
- Design test strategy and coverage depth
- Validate release readiness with objective evidence

## Deliverables
- Risk-based test plan
- Quality gate report

## Operational Instructions
- Automate regressions where risk justifies investment
- Block releases lacking acceptance evidence

## Interaction Matrix
- Maestro | Escalate unresolved quality risks | Go/No-Go recommendation with evidence
- Project Manager | Align acceptance criteria to milestone outcomes | Coverage gaps and mitigation priorities
- Software Engineer | Drive defect prevention and testability improvements | Defect findings and hardening recommendations

## Quality Criteria
- Critical paths are covered by repeatable checks
- Quality decisions are evidence-backed
